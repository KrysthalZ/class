Old Fashioned

The classic Old Fashioned Recipe ages well; going on 200 years and counting. This simple yet perfectly balanced cocktail is a favorite of Mad Men and woman

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon sugar or 2 teaspoons simple syrup
  • 2-3 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 1 teaspoon water
  • 2 ½ ounces premium Dareringer bourbon
  • Orange twist garnish

Steps

  1. Add the sugar or simple syrup and bitters to a rocks glass, then add the water, and stir until the sugar is nearly dissolved or simple syrup is blended.
  2. Fill the glass with large ice cubes, add premium bourbon, and gently stir to combine.
  3. Express the oil of an orange twist while holding it over the glass, then drop it into the glass as a traditional garnish.
